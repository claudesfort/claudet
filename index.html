Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025 | AI Ascend is committed to protecting your confidential information.

1. Information we collect

We collect business contact information (name, title, company, email, phone), consultation notes, and project-related data shared by clients. We do not collect sensitive personal data without explicit consent.

2. How we use your information

To provide consulting services, deliver training, communicate about projects, invoice, and improve our offerings. Client data is never used to train public models or shared with third parties.

3. Confidentiality

All client discussions, proprietary data, and business strategies are protected by NDAs and strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Data retention

Project data retained for the duration of engagement plus 12 months unless deletion is requested. You may request data export or deletion at any time.

5. Third-party sharing

We use trusted tools (CRM, email, project management) under data processing agreements. We never sell or rent personal information.

6. Your rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability. Email privacy@aiascend.com. You may also opt out of marketing communications.

7. Security measures

Encrypted storage, access controls, regular security training, and SOC 2 aligned practices.

8. Children's privacy

Our services are for businesses and professionals only. Not for individuals under 18.

9. Policy updates & contact

Material changes will be communicated via email. Data Protection Officer: dpo@aiascend.com. Address: 101 AI Boulevard, Suite 300, Austin, TX 78701.
